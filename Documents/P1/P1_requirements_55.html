Our Client: Café Saldo

Café Saldo is a café for students in Trondheim. The goal for the café is to make it easy for students to order and eat healthy and good food, without robbing them for all their lunch-money.The prices at the café is what we can call nice, and the food is even better.

Most of the food they provide consists of greens and are freshly made. They want a webpage to better communicate their service and tempt customers to try more of the different recipes.

What is the motivation for the target group to visit the website?

The target group would want to visit the website to get information about the different lunch-alternatives the restaurant provides, such as gluten free, lactose free and vegetarian alternatives.

How will they get what they seek?

Visiting the website visitors can see various offerings and information by navigating between pages through an intuitive menu.

Is there more than one kind of user?

The main segment here is students, but the website and the concept are also designed for teachers and staff at the university.

Functional and non-functional requirements:

Functional requirements:

  • Contact information of the café
  • Mailadress or the phone number of the café
  • Show what meals or drinks that are available as vegetarian options, gluten free options, lactose free options and non-vegetarian options.
  • Opening hours of the café

Non-Functional requirments:

  • Redirecting between pages should take less than 2 seconds.
  • The site should allow multiple users at once
  • The site should be easily navigated on laptop and handheld devices
  • The site should be compatible with Chrome and Safari web browsers
  • The site should give visible feedback for all user actions

Content of the website:

1. Information:

Some information about the concept and the segment the café wants to cover. The info-part will also consist of information about the benefits of healthy food for students with a lot on their plates.

2. Different lunch alternatives:

The customer will have to choose type of lunch. The alternatives could be:

  • Salad
  • Sandwich
  • Smoothie

3. The site should provide information about allergens, preferences or recipes that have options to replace ingredients to accommodate these.

The website should indicate options to avoid certain contents such as gluten, lactose, non-vegetarian or vegan ingredients. Menu items that allow customization should have non intrusive symbols that indicate the different options.

Final location of the website​: Easy and memorable domain name

Specific preferences:

  • The website should have a clean and “fresh” look that underline the healthy theme of the café.
  • The pictures have to be clear and welcoming.
  • The price range should be visible when opening the website.
  • The website has to be easily navigated on laptops and handheld devices.
